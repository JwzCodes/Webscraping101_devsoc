## Free practice challenges (do any, in any order)

Pick whatever looks fun. Not all challenges will have solutions provided

1. **Cheapest book.** Modify exercise 4 so it prints only the single cheapest book
   on page 1. (Hint: strip the `£`, convert to `float`, track the minimum.)

2. **Five-star only.** Print only the books on page 1 that have a `"Five"` rating.

3. **One category.** Scrape every book in any category and count them.
   Then swap the URL for another category from the sidebar.
   (See `bonus_01_by_category.py`.)

4. **Detail pages.** For the first book, follow its link to the detail page and
   print the description and its UPC from the product information table.
   (See `bonus_02_book_details.py`.)

5. **Analyse it.** After running the capstone, use pandas to find the average
   price and the count of books per rating.
